Previously, I received my Ph.D. from Networked Systems Lab (NSL) at University of Southern California, advised by Prof. Ramesh Govindan. Before joining NSL, I obtained my B.S degree in Department of Electrical Engineering, Shanghai Jiao Tong University, advised by Prof. Xinbing Wang.
